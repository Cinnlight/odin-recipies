Ultimate Potato Soup

A thick, rich potato soup that is both high in calories and flavor.

This is a great party meal that serves about 8 people.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound bacon, chopped
  • 2 stalks celery, diced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 8 potatoes, peeled and cubed
